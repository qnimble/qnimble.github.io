Compliance

FCC#

The Quarto is classified as an unintentional radiator and it is FCC compliant by exemption (Part 15, Equipment Authorization Exemptions, section 3c). See FCC Part 15 Equipment Authorization Exemption for details. The Quarto is powered by an external power supply that is UL certified for US and Canada.

Industry Canada#

The Quarto is classified as an unintentional radiator and it is ICES-003 compliant by exemption (1.5.1 General Exemptions, section d). See ICES-003 Information Technology Equipment (including Digital Apparatus) for details. The Quarto is powered by an external power supply that is UL certified for US and Canada.

RoHS#

The Quarto is RoHS compliant and exclusively uses RoHS-compliant components.
