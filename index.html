qNimble Quarto

A Digital Brain for the Analog World™

Super Low Latency

Read from the DAC, write to the ADC and do floating point math, all with a latency of few microseconds. Enables servo bandwidths of up to 100kHz. Get the speed of an FPGA without the complexity.

Lab-grade Analog Measurements

16-bit DAC and ADC, +/-10V range with 1 MSPS data rate and low-drift analog references for precision measurements.

Compatible with Arduino™

Easy to program in the Arduino™ IDE. Write your code, click 'upload' and your code is running on the Quarto. Compatible with most C compilers / development environments.
